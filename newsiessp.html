Newsies Sneak Preview -- 8th Grade

I am so excited to perform this show as the third shift and closing main character, Jack Kelly. Jack is the leader of the Manhatten newsies and the leader of the Newsboy Strike of 1899. Jack dreams of escaping New York to Santa Fe with his best friend, Crutchie, but once the price of newspapers are raised by Joseph Pulitzer, he takes it upon himself to lead a legendary strike.
